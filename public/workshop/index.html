About the event

It is the age of automation in all fields of technology. Smart cars and i-automobiles are a trending topic in the industry. Get ready to immerse yourself in the den of Teslas and Google cars.

The workshop aims to address the falling standards in our coursework by including hands own sessions on various topics and introduce passionate students to the industry standards.

IET On Campus CET is proud to present before you Smart Rover Workshop using NodeMCU. NodeMCU is a development platform which is suitable for beginers, professionals and hobbyists alike. NodeMCU brings the easiness of using an Arduino as well as the convenience of an onboard WiFi module for communication. Thus it is an optimal package for beginers to delve into embedded systems as well as IOT.
